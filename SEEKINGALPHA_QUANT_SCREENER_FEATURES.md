## 7. DATA SOURCES & CALCULATIONS

### Quant Rating Calculation
Combines 5 factors with proprietary weighting:
1. **Valuation** (~20%) - Relative value metrics
2. **Growth** (~20%) - Revenue/EPS growth trends
3. **Profitability** (~20%) - Margin/return metrics
4. **Momentum** (~20%) - Price momentum signals
5. **EPS Revisions** (~20%) - Analyst estimate changes

### Grade Calculation
- Percentile ranking within sector/industry
- Historical comparison (Now vs 3M vs 6M)
- Relative to market average
